 Rules:
- The goal of the game is to Flip the opponent pieces and make made the join your side or Capture (remove) them from the board till the opponent have no pieces left.
- Player must decide which color they should pick, The Black Dogs, or, The White Dogs and this is very important because The White Dogs start first.
- After picking the color, The Black Dogs go on the White squares and move on them only, while The White Dogs go on the Black squares and move on them only.
- To Capture or Flip an opponent piece There must be Two pieces from the same color connected from their perspective square corners (diagonally) and both are connected to the opponent piece from each side of that piece.
- The Flipped piece, should turn to match the player's pieces color and move it to the closest legal square color that could move on.
